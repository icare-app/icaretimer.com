Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use iCare without logging in?

Yes. However, you won't have access to features such as custom sound notiifications & tracking your data usage.

How does iCare take measures to protect my data?

You can enable/disable tracking data usage in the Preferences menu. Additionally, user's data is deleted weekly from the database.

How is iCare different than other smart timers?

Extensive features & customizability through timer-blocking apps, custom notification sounds, and viewing your timer & app usage.
